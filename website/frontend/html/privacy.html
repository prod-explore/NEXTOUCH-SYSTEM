Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

Nextouch is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Data Collection

Nextouch does not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics
  • Device identifiers (beyond local license validation)
  • Location data
  • Any form of telemetry

Local Communication

All communication between the mobile app and desktop server happens entirely over your local WiFi network. No data is sent to the internet during normal operation.

License Validation

When activating a Pro license, the desktop app sends a one-time request to our server containing only:

  • Your license key
  • A machine identifier (hash of hardware info)

This is used solely to validate your purchase and prevent unauthorized use. No personal data is collected.

Advertisements

The free version displays ads via Google AdMob. Google's privacy policy applies to ad-related data collection. Pro users see no ads.

Third-Party Services

  • Stripe — Payment processing. See Stripe's Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — Ads in free version. See Google's Privacy Policy
